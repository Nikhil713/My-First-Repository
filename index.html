ONE PIECE

After everything that Luffy did in the entirety of the summit War Saga, Luffy's bounty only raised by 100 Million Beri. And while yeah that is alot, it's not nearly enough when you factor in all his actions against the WG itself. I mean he defeated another Shichibukai, punched a world noble (A crime in the OP world that is greater than mass murder) ,broke into Impel Down and led the only mass escape in it's history, had his relationship to Dragon revealed and participated in the Marineford war. Making him the only man to infiltrate all 3 main WG bases. So after all this only 100 million beri seems really really small. Now i know Oda couldn't have Luffy's bounting getting to high at that point in the series, but come on. Even after beating Doffy Luffy's Bounty still hasn't even matched Ace's . So what gives? Give Luffy his due Oda.
